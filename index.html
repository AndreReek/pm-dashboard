How it works

From signup to first project in 5 minutes

1

Create your account

Register with your work email. Your organization is created automatically — no IT setup, no invite codes, no waiting. A demo project is ready to explore the moment you log in.

2

Add your first real project

Enter the project name, client, supply scope (technologies and brands), and a target end date. The 19-step workflow is pre-loaded and ready to use.

3

Open the Step Timeline

Click the 📅 icon on any project. Set working-day durations for each pending step. The dashboard calculates your estimated close week automatically — W14, W22, whatever it is — and flags the health status based on your target date.

4

Track and update as you go

Check off workflow steps as they complete. Update supply statuses. Adjust step durations when timelines shift. The health score recalculates instantly — no manual RAG updates.
