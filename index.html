Setup in 5 minutes

From signup to live project in under 5 minutes

1

Sign up with your work email

Your organization is created automatically — no IT setup, no invite codes, no waiting. A demo project is ready to explore the moment you log in.

2

Add your first project

Enter the project name, client, supply scope (technologies and brands), and a target end date. The 19-step workflow is pre-loaded and ready to use.

3

Set your step durations

Click the 📅 icon on any project. Set working-day durations for each pending step. Fieldline calculates your estimated close week automatically — W14, W22, whatever it is — and flags the health status based on your target date.

4

Update as you go — health scores recalculate automatically

Check off workflow steps as they complete. Update supply statuses. Adjust step durations when timelines shift. The health score recalculates instantly — no manual RAG updates.
